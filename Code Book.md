## Labels of various activities during tests

* `WALKING`(`1`): walking
* `WALKING_UPSTAIRS`(`2`): walking on a stairs
* `WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS`(`3`):walking down a stairs
* `SITTING`(`4`): sitting
* `STANDING`(`5`): standing
* `LAYING`(`6`): lying down
